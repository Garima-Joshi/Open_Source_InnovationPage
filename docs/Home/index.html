What is DevSphere?


DevSphere is a developer-centric platform designed to foster collaboration, learning, and growth within the open-source community. We provide a space where developers from all backgrounds can connect, collaborate on projects, and elevate their coding journey.

This project is proudly hosted and nurtured under the esteemed banner of the AWS Cloud Club at IGDTUW, a hub of technology enthusiasts, visionaries, and change-makers.
